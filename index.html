Marbury Road Edible Garden

A Voluntary Community Market Garden & Wellness Hub

Born out of a need for food security in Stockport, the group have cultivated a space for the local community to learn, to grow and harvest crops freely!

Marbury Road Edible Garden is a volunteer-run community group focusing on gardening and growing food for the community. We also run wellbeing workshops, including yoga, mindful watercolour painting, healing meditation and more!
